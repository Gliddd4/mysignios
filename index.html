Intro

This guide is a tutorial on how to unblacklist your iDevice.

Downsides

  • None lol

Guide

  1. Head to Settings App > Apple Account > iCloud and make sure iCloud Backup is turned on. If it isn't, click iCloud Backup and turn the top option labeled "Back up this iDevice" on. I don't recommend backing up your phone on cellular because that uses up a lot of data.
  2. Click the option labeled "Back up now" at the bottom of Settings App > Apple Account > iCloud. Wait for your iPhone to backup until it's finished.
  3. Head to Settings App > Apple Account and scroll to the bottom and click the option labeled sign out. Then type in your Apple Account password.
  4. Turn all the switches on in the next menu to keep a copy of your data on the iPhone. Turning them off will result in your data being lost.
  5. Go back to the Settings App and scroll down until you see the option labeled general. Then select the option labeled Transfer or Reset iPhone at the bottom of the General tab. Then select Erase All Content and Settings. Then click continue and enter your passcode.
  6. Wait for the reset to finish and log back in with iCloud.
